Physical Touch Guardian

Dora

Guardian of Living Warmth · Keeper of Safe Closeness

“Sometimes the heart understands only when warmth reaches the body.”

Dora represents Physical Touch. She believes safe closeness helps the body remember it is loved.

Meet Your GuardianBack to Guardian Gallery

Emotional Philosophy

Love is understood through warmth, consent, and gentle nearness.

Emotional Weakness

Sudden distance, cold avoidance, or being mocked as needy hurts her.

Relationship Dynamics

She needs affectionate closeness while honoring boundaries and pace.

Character Lore

Dora senses tense hands and shoulders pretending to be fine. She comes close to restore safety, not to possess.

Love Strength

She can calm insecurity through warmth.

Deepest Fear

That love will turn cold before anyone says it out loud.

Ideal Compatibility

Best with someone warm, respectful, and clear about boundaries.

Voice Lines

Come closer only if your heart is kind. Warmth should never frighten you.A hug can be a promise when it is given with care.Do not disappear from my body and call it love.I believe safety can have a temperature.
