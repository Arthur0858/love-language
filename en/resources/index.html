How to choose supplies

Choose one supply that fits the current moment. Do not buy or read everything at once. If the relationship involves danger, control, violence, or acute pressure, seek trusted people and professional support first.

When not to buy

Pause if you want a product to replace an apology, force someone to change, or soothe a purchase impulse. LoveTypes supplies are for reflection and practice.

Core text

The 5 Love Languages

Gary Chapman

The source text behind the love-language framework and a useful bridge back from the five guardians.

Best for: Readers meeting love languages for the first time.

Use with: Translate examples into your own culture, tone, and relationship context.
