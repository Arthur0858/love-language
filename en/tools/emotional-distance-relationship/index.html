Name the form of distance

Sometimes love is still present but hidden under fatigue, pressure, or cross-signals.

FAQ

Does emotional distance mean they stopped loving me?

Not necessarily. This page does not make love verdicts; it names distance and one repair step to try.

What if they keep avoiding me?

Ask for one small, clear step first. If avoidance continues, keep your own boundaries visible.

Is this for married or long-term partners?

Yes. Long-term relationships often lose visible care under routines and stress.
