FAQ

Should we apologize or cool down first?

Start by naming a safe cooldown window, then reopen with one small request. The compass helps make the sentence easier to receive.

Can I use it when they left me on read?

Yes. Instead of treating it as proof they do not care, use the compass to identify what you needed in that moment.

Will it decide whether long distance can work?

No. LoveTypes does not make breakup or destiny verdicts. It maps the next repair step.
