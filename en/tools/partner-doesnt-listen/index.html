FAQ

Why does my partner still not listen after I say it many times?

It may be pressure defense, distraction, love-language mismatch, or unwillingness to own impact. The compass names the pattern first.

Should I keep repeating until they get it?

Usually no. Reduce it to one main point and one answerable request, then observe whether they return to dialogue.

What if they always deny my feelings?

Long-term denial, mockery, humiliation, or control is not just a communication technique problem. Put your safety and support first.
