FAQ

How is this different from a dating compatibility quiz?

LoveTypes does not stop at a label. It turns cross-signals into a repair step you can practice.

Do both people need to take it together?

You can do it together, or start with your own guardian and current relationship status.

Does a low score mean we are incompatible?

No. This page does not make breakup verdicts. It points to what needs translation and repair.
